Kaitlyn Steffy, 10, of Woodinville, sits with the family dog, Tina, this morning before the Super Bowl Parade at 11 a.m. The Seahawks will Ride the Ducks down Fourth Avenue and could draw as many as 500,000 people to downtown Seattle. Crowds have already snarled traffic and public transportation, with major delays reported on buses, ferries and the light rail.
